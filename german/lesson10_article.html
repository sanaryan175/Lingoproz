Lesson 10: 🗣️ Everyday Conversations in German

Master daily expressions to confidently interact in real-life situations.

💬 Common Everyday Phrases

Below are useful German phrases with word-by-word meanings to help you understand sentence structure:

  • 😊 Wie geht’s?
    How are you?
    🧩 Wie = how, geht’s = (it) goes
    (Literally: "How goes it?")
  • 🙋 Ich heiße Anna.
    My name is Anna.
    🧩 Ich = I, heiße = am called, Anna = Anna
    (Literally: "I am called Anna.")
  • 🤝 Schön dich zu treffen.
    Nice to meet you.
    🧩 Schön = nice, dich = you, zu treffen = to meet
  • 🏠 Wo wohnst du?
    Where do you live?
    🧩 Wo = where, wohnst = live (you), du = you
  • 🌏 Ich komme aus Indien.
    I am from India.
    🧩 Ich = I, komme = come, aus = from, Indien = India
  • 📘 Ich lerne Deutsch.
    I’m learning German.
    🧩 Ich = I, lerne = learn, Deutsch = German
  • ❓ Ich verstehe nicht.
    I don’t understand.
    🧩 Ich = I, verstehe = understand, nicht = not
  • 🐢 Bitte sprechen Sie langsam.
    Please speak slowly.
    🧩 Bitte = please, sprechen = speak, Sie = you (formal), langsam = slowly
  • 🗣️ Sprechen Sie Englisch?
    Do you speak English?
    🧩 Sprechen = speak, Sie = you (formal), Englisch = English
  • 🆘 Können Sie mir helfen?
    Can you help me?
    🧩 Können = can, Sie = you (formal), mir = me, helfen = help
  • 📝 Ich habe eine Frage.
    I have a question.
    🧩 Ich = I, habe = have, eine = a, Frage = question

🧠 Easy Grammar & Sentence Tips

  • “Wie geht’s?” means “How are you?” (casual)
    👉 Use “Wie geht es Ihnen?” to sound polite or when talking to strangers/adults.
  • 🧩 German sentences usually follow the S-V-O pattern:
    👉 Subject – Verb – Object (just like English!)
    Example: Ich liebe Deutsch. (I love German.)
  • ⚙️ Modal verbs (like können = can) go in the second position.
    The main action verb goes to the end.
    Example: Können Sie mir helfen?
    (Can you help me?)
  • 👋 Ich heiße... means “I am called...”
    👉 It’s how you say your name in German!
    Example: Ich heiße Anna. = I’m Anna.

💡 Did You Know?

  • 👋 “Wie geht’s?” means “How are you?” — it’s used with friends.
    👉 For polite or formal situations, say: “Wie geht es Ihnen?”
  • 🙋‍♂️ “Ich heiße...” means “My name is...” or “I’m called...”.
    👉 Example: Ich heiße Max. = My name is Max.
  • 🇩🇪 Germans speak clearly and directly.
    👉 Learn polite phrases like:
    “Können Sie mir helfen?” = Can you help me?
    “Ich verstehe nicht.” = I don’t understand.
  • 🗣️ Don’t worry if you make mistakes!
    👉 Say: “Bitte sprechen Sie langsam.”
    (Please speak slowly.)
    It shows you're trying, and people will appreciate that.
  • ❗ “Entschuldigung” means both “Sorry” and “Excuse me”.
    👉 Very useful when you're in shops or on the street.
