Lesson 5: Days, Months & Seasons in German

Master the German words for days of the week, months of the year, and the four seasons!

📅 Days of the Week (Wochentage)

  • Montag – Monday
  • Dienstag – Tuesday
  • Mittwoch – Wednesday
  • Donnerstag – Thursday
  • Freitag – Friday
  • Samstag – Saturday
  • Sonntag – Sunday

🗓️ Months of the Year (Monate)

  • Januar – January
  • Februar – February
  • März – March
  • April – April
  • Mai – May
  • Juni – June
  • Juli – July
  • August – August
  • September – September
  • Oktober – October
  • November – November
  • Dezember – December

🌤️ Seasons (Jahreszeiten)

  • der Frühling – Spring
  • der Sommer – Summer
  • der Herbst – Autumn / Fall
  • der Winter – Winter

💬 Usage in Context

Heute ist Montag.
Today is Monday.
🗓️ “Heute” = today

Mein Geburtstag ist im Juli.
My birthday is in July.
🎉 “im” is used for months and seasons → “im Juli” , “im Sommer”

Im Herbst sind die Blätter bunt.
In autumn, the leaves are colorful.
🍂 “bunt” = colorful, “Blätter” = leaves

Wir fahren im Winter in die Berge.
We go to the mountains in winter.
🏔️ “fahren” = travel/go by vehicle, “Berge” = mountains

🧠 Did You Know?

  • Days and months are not capitalized in English, but they are in German!
  • All seasons in German are masculine nouns → der Frühling , Sommer , Herbst , Winter
  • The preposition “im” is short for “in dem” and is used with months and seasons.
