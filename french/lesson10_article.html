Lesson 10: 🗣️ Everyday Conversations in French

Learn how to talk in daily life – greetings, questions, and more – in simple French!

💬 Common Everyday Phrases

These are common French phrases explained word-by-word for beginners:

  • 😊 Comment ça va ?
    How are you?
    🧩 Comment = how, ça = it/that, va = goes
    (Literally: "How is it going?")
  • 🙋 Je m'appelle Anna.
    My name is Anna.
    🧩 Je = I, m'appelle = call myself, Anna = Anna
    (Literally: "I call myself Anna.")
  • 🤝 Enchanté(e) de vous rencontrer.
    Nice to meet you.
    🧩 Enchanté(e) = pleased, de = to, vous = you (formal), rencontrer = meet
  • 🏠 Où habites-tu ?
    Where do you live?
    🧩 Où = where, habites = live (you), tu = you (informal)
  • 🌏 Je viens d'Inde.
    I come from India.
    🧩 Je = I, viens = come, de = from, l'Inde = India
  • 📘 J'apprends le français.
    I am learning French.
    🧩 Je = I, apprends = learn, le français = French
  • ❓ Je ne comprends pas.
    I don’t understand.
    🧩 Je = I, ne + pas = not, comprends = understand
  • 🐢 Parlez lentement, s'il vous plaît.
    Please speak slowly.
    🧩 Parlez = speak, lentement = slowly, s'il vous plaît = please
  • 🗣️ Parlez-vous anglais ?
    Do you speak English?
    🧩 Parlez = speak, vous = you (formal), anglais = English
  • 🆘 Pouvez-vous m'aider ?
    Can you help me?
    🧩 Pouvez = can (you), vous = you, m' = me, aider = help
  • 📝 J'ai une question.
    I have a question.
    🧩 J' = I, ai = have, une = a, question = question

🧠 Easy Grammar & Sentence Tips

  • “Comment ça va ?” is used informally.
    👉 Say “Comment allez-vous ?” when you're being polite.
  • 🧩 French follows S-V-O order:
    👉 Subject – Verb – Object (like English)
    Example: J'aime le français. = I love French.
  • ⚙️ Negative sentences use ne ... pas around the verb:
    Example: Je ne comprends pas. = I don’t understand.
  • 👋 Je m'appelle... is how to say your name.
    Example: Je m'appelle Emma. = I’m Emma.

💡 Did You Know?

  • 🙋‍♀️ “Je m'appelle...” = “My name is...” or “I call myself...”
  • 📣 French pronunciation is smooth. Practice with slow speech first: “Parlez lentement, s'il vous plaît.”
  • 🇫🇷 People appreciate when you try. Even if you make mistakes, smile and say: “Je suis débutant(e).” = I’m a beginner.
  • ❗ “Excusez-moi” means “Excuse me”.
    👉 Very helpful in shops or when asking strangers.
